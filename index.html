Welcome to the Digital Tidepool

This is a simulation based on the Game of Life created by the late John Conway in the seventies. The game of life is a no-player game where a simple set of rules and starting conditions lead to complex and unpredicable interactions downstream. It's a really interesting idea and my greatest single inspiration for creating the Digital Tidepool. If you're interested in the developmental process behind this project, visit my blog.
